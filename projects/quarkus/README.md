# Logo Assets

There are two alignments of the Quarkus logo (vertical and horizontal), use either depending on space requirements.

> **Background Color** Notice there are multiple versions of logo coloring for use on different background colors (light and dark options). The "default" logo is for use on a light background while the "reverse" logo is for use on dark backgrounds. The one-color versions are designed for use when you have to present it monochromatically.

# Logo Do's and Don't

The key to keeping the Quarkus brand strong is consistency. We do this by maintaining a uniform way of presenting the logo across all mediums over time. This means we need to respect the spacing requirements and supplied color options.

## Logo Spacing

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/commonhaus/artwork/refs/heads/main/projects/quarkus/images/logo_spacing_vert.png" alt="Use proper spacing around vertical logo">
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/commonhaus/artwork/refs/heads/main/projects/quarkus/images/logo_spacing_hori.png" alt="Use proper spacing around horizontal logo">

The logo needs space to breath so make sure you leave at least an icon's height and width around it.

## What NOT to do

Make sure you use the logo correctly and don't do silly stuff like...

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/commonhaus/artwork/refs/heads/main/projects/quarkus/images/logo_recolor.png" alt="Don't recolor the logo">

Don't use other colors in the logo.

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/commonhaus/artwork/refs/heads/main/projects/quarkus/images/logo_resize.png" alt="Don't resize elements of the logo">

Don't resize individual elements of the logo.

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/commonhaus/artwork/refs/heads/main/projects/quarkus/images/logo_skew.png" alt="Don't skew the logo">

Don't squish or strech the logo to change it's proportions.

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/commonhaus/artwork/refs/heads/main/projects/quarkus/images/logo_dropshadow.png" alt="Don't put a dropshadow on the logo">

Don't add a dropshadow effect on the logo on a white background.

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/commonhaus/artwork/refs/heads/main/projects/quarkus/images/logo_gradient.png" alt="Don't add a gradient to the logo">

Don't add gradient effects to interior of the logo.

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/commonhaus/artwork/refs/heads/main/projects/quarkus/images/logo_logotype.png" alt="Don't use the logotype by itself">

Don't use the logotype by itself.

# Color Palette

### Primary Colors

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/commonhaus/artwork/refs/heads/main/projects/quarkus/images/colorpalette_primary.png" alt="Primary color palette">

The Triad of Quarkus brand colors: Quarkus Blue, Dark Blue and Red. These colors are used most broadly in building visual associations with Quarkus.


- Quarkus Blue (#4695EB) Pantone Process Blue U
- Quarkus Dark Blue (#0D1C2C) Pantone 3035 U
- Quarkus Red (#FF004A) Pantone 032 U

### Secondary Colors

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/commonhaus/artwork/refs/heads/main/projects/quarkus/images/colorpalette_secondary.png" alt="Secondary color palette">

The secondary colors extend the range of colors available for use in graphs, charts and other limited uses.

- Quarkus Orange (#F18F01)
- Quarkus Green (#6AB443)
- Quarkus Purple (#6622CC)
